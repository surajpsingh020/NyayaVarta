# NyayaVarta - Project Plan

## Project Overview
NyayaVarta is a legal literacy platform aimed at empowering diverse communities with accurate and accessible legal information, interactive learning modules, and real-time legal aid support.

## Objectives
- Provide multilingual legal content.
- Offer bite-sized learning modules with quizzes and gamification.
- Connect users with legal aid services in real time.
- Engage users with community forums and real-time legal updates.

## Target Audience
- Students
- Professionals
- Senior Citizens

## Key Features

- **Multilingual Support:**  
  Legal content will be available in multiple languages with accurate translation and localization.

- **Interactive Learning Modules:**  
  Bite-sized lessons equipped with quizzes, scenarios, and gamification (badges, progress tracking).

- **User Profiles:**  
  Custom-tailored content for different user types (students, professionals, senior citizens).

- **Real-Time Legal Aid:**  
  Chatbot integration and a directory of legal aid services for instant support.

- **Legal Updates:**  
  Automated scraping to fetch and notify about the latest legal news and policies.

- **Community Forums:**  
  A platform for users to engage in discussions and share experiences.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:**  
  React.js for a dynamic and responsive UI.

- **Backend:**  
  Django (Python) for its robust features and rapid development capabilities.

- **Database:**  
  MySQL for structured and scalable data storage.

- **Localization Tools:**  
  i18next (or Weblate) to support multilingual content.

- **Hosting:**  
  AWS Free Tier (EC2) for hosting the platform.

- **Additional Tools:**  
  BeautifulSoup (Python) for web scraping legal updates and Rasa (or similar) for chatbot integration.
